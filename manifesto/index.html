PushType manifesto

The PushType mission is to inspire the next generation of Ruby developers with a next generation content management system.

1. We are makers

Be creative and embrace your ideas. Think big, start small. Be a hacker and a fixer. Obsessively improve things.

2. We are discoverers

Do the things you've never done. Seek different ways. Don't be afraid to fail. Never stop learning.

3. We are different

Be the crazy one. Nurture your curiosity. Believe in yourself. Don't assume others are never wrong.

4. We are community

Embrace our diversity and never discriminate. Reach out and participate. Share your ideas, enthusiasm and skills. Support one another, be friendly and have fun.

5. We are Rubyists

Enjoy programming and strive to be happy. Write beautiful code. Build wonderful things.
